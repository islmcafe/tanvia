About Me

Research Interests

  • Health Informatics
  • Information Behavior
  • Knowledge Management
  • Library and Information Science
  • Misinformation Studies
  • Social Media Analysis
  • Scientometrics

My research focuses on combating misinformation in health informatics, social media, and online platforms. I analyze user information behavior and knowledge management to develop strategies for improving information accuracy and reliability. Additionally, I explore how libraries and information science can support effective knowledge systems and promote responsible information sharing.

As an Assistant Cataloguer at the Islamic University of Technology (IUT), I lead digital transformation initiatives and manage comprehensive library systems. My work spans from modernizing library infrastructure to conducting extensive research on information behavior and digital literacy.
