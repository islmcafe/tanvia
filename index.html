About Me

Exploring the intersections of technology, humanities, and creativity

I am currently pursuing my Master of Arts (Digital Humanities) at the University of Alberta, where I combine academic research, technology, and creativity. My journey began with a Bachelor's in Information Science & Library Management at the University of Dhaka, which gave me strong foundations in knowledge systems, digital tools, and archival practices.

I've worked in academia, NGOs, and cultural institutions, developing expertise in data analysis, digital knowledge management, and community engagement. My creative edge lies in graphic design, visualization, and 2D/3D modeling, making me passionate about bridging technical and artistic perspectives.

My research focuses on the ethical and philosophical dimensions of AI, particularly in creative domains, while exploring how digital humanities can foster more inclusive and accessible knowledge systems.
